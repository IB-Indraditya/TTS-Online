Frequently Asked Questions

What is T T S Online?
T T S Online is a free online tool for providing text to speech service to the user via voicerss and you can play or download audio files.
Can I use T T S Online for commercial use?
Yes, you have 100% copyright ownership of the audio files and can use them for any purpose, including commercial use, as long as it complies with local laws.
How does it work?
First enter the text or upload file, then select the language and voice type, and finally do the conversion. Visit Utilities page for deatils.
Is text-to-speech free to use?
Yes, we will offer a permanent free version for users, reserving the right to make adjustments to relevant policies in the future.
